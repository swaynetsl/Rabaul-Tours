Explore East New Britain

Discover the best of Rabaul and Kokopo through our guided day trips and island adventures. Whether you want history, volcanoes, or beach escapes, we have a tour for everyone.

Bitapaka War Cemetery Tour

Visit the beautifully kept Bitapaka War Cemetery near Kokopo, a peaceful memorial honoring soldiers of World War I & II. Learn about the history of Rabaul and its wartime stories on this meaningful tour.

Mount Tavurvur & Hot Springs Adventure

Experience the thrill of visiting an active volcano! See Mount Tavurvur up close, watch steam rise from the Rabaul Hot Springs, and capture stunning views of the surrounding caldera. A perfect mix of nature and adventure.

Pangpang Island Day Trip (Duke of York)

Enjoy a scenic boat ride to Pangpang Island in the Duke of York group. Swim, snorkel, relax on white sands, and experience local village life a true island paradise getaway.

Rabaul Town & Historical Sites Tour

Explore the ruins of old Rabaul, visit the Japanese tunnels, volcano observatory, and bustling markets. Learn how this historic town was shaped by volcanic eruptions and wartime events.
