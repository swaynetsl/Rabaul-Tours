Tavurvur Hike

Visit one of Papua New Guinea’s most famous active volcanoes. Mount Tavurvur offers stunning views, exciting guided hikes, and dramatic scenery a must-see for every visitor to Rabaul.

Hotspring

Feel the earth’s power at Rabaul’s natural hot springs! Watch steam rise from bubbling pools, see locals cook in the geothermal heat, and enjoy views of the surrounding volcanic landscape.

Bitapaka War Cemetery

Step into history at this peaceful memorial site near Kokopo, where rows of white headstones honor soldiers who served in World War I & II. A beautifully maintained place for reflection and remembrance.

Pangpang Island

Escape to the calm turquoise waters and soft sands of Pangpang Island. Relax, snorkel, and experience warm local hospitality in this island paradise just a short boat ride from Kokopo.
