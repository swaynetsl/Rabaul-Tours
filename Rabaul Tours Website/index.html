Welcome to Rabaul Tours Your Local Adventure Awaits!

Our team isn’t just here to guide you we’re here to welcome you like family. You’ll hear stories from local life, taste fresh island food, and see Rabaul through the eyes of the people who call it home. We believe the best travel memories come from real connections. Step into the heart of East New Britain, where volcanoes rise from the sea, islands sparkle under the sun, and history whispers through the hills. At Rabaul Tours, we’re here to share our home the incredible beauty, culture, and stories of Rabaul, Papua New Guinea. We’re not a big company we’re locals who grew up here, who love this land deeply, and who take pride in showing visitors what makes it so special. Whether it’s watching the sunrise over Mount Tavurvur, soaking in the Matupit hot springs, exploring the historic Bitapaka War Cemetery, or spending a day on Pangpang Island at the Duke of York Islands, every tour is designed to give you an unforgettable experience. smiling faces, shared laughter, and the feeling that you’ve truly been part of something. So whether you’re an adventurer, a history lover, or just looking to relax by the sea, Rabaul Tours has something for you. Come and explore our home, meet our people, and let us show you the magic of East New Britain. Your journey begins here let’s explore Rabaul together!

Featured Tours

  • Mount Tavurvur Volcano Adventure - Hike up one of the most active volcanoes in PNG and witness breathtaking views of Rabaul.
  • Hot Springs & Local Village Tour - Visit the famous Matupit hot springs and meet the friendly locals who live nearby.
  • Bitapaka War Cemetery Tour - Discover the rich WWII history that shaped Rabaul and pay respects at this peaceful memorial site.
  • Pangpang Island Escape - A relaxing day of swimming, snorkeling, and island fun at the Duke of York Islands.
