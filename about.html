About Rabaul Tours

Welcome to Rabaul Tours, your friendly local guide to exploring the wonders of East New Britain, Papua New Guinea! We are a proudly local-owned tour company based in Rabaul, dedicated to sharing the beauty, culture, and history of our amazing province with travelers from around the world. Our goal is to make every visitor feel at home while discovering the unforgettable sights, sounds, and stories that make this part of PNG so special. From the moment you arrive, our passionate local guides will take you on exciting adventures across Rabaul and the Gazelle Peninsula from hiking the active Mount Tavurvur volcano and relaxing at the hot springs of Matupit, to visiting the Bitapaka War Cemetery, a place of deep historical significance. We also offer day trips to the stunning Duke of York Islands, where you can swim, snorkel, or simply unwind on the white sandy beaches of Pangpang Island. At Rabaul Tours, we believe that travel is more than just sightseeing it’s about connecting with people, culture, and nature. That’s why we make sure every tour is personal, safe, and filled with genuine local hospitality. Our guides are not just experts in the area they are storytellers who will share the legends, traditions, and history passed down through generations. Whether you’re an adventurer seeking volcano hikes, a history lover exploring WWII relics, or someone just wanting to experience the island lifestyle, Rabaul Tours has something special for you. We tailor our experiences to suit your interests and time, so you can see the very best of East New Britain at your own pace. Come join us and discover the heart of Rabaul a place where fiery volcanoes meet turquoise seas, history lives in every corner, and warm smiles welcome you everywhere you go.
